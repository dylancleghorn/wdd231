NP Membership Benefits

NP membership is focused on service and community impact for qualifying non-profit organizations.

  • Listing in the online chamber directory as a non-profit partner.
  • Access to select community events and service opportunities.
  • Discounted rates on training and educational workshops.
  • Opportunities to collaborate with local businesses and civic groups.

Bronze Membership Benefits

Bronze is a great starting point for small businesses wanting visibility and connection.

  • Directory listing with contact information and website link.
  • Invitations to monthly networking events.
  • Member-only pricing for chamber-hosted trainings and luncheons.
  • Business news highlights in quarterly email newsletters.

Silver Membership Benefits

Silver memberships add more promotion and event visibility for your organization.

  • All Bronze level benefits.
  • Periodic spotlight position on the home page directory.
  • Logo placement on select chamber event materials.
  • Priority access to training and leadership roundtables.

Gold Membership Benefits

Gold membership provides premier recognition and partnership opportunities with the Chamber.

  • All Silver level benefits.
  • Regular hero or spotlight features on the chamber site.
  • Event sponsorship opportunities and stage recognition.
  • Exclusive strategic planning sessions with chamber leadership.
